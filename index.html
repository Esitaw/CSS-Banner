5 Turn ons when looking for a partner

  1. She must be career-driven in life.
  2. All I want is for her to treat me with kindness in spite of my imperfections.
  3. She should be a hygienic person and have a pleasant smell.
  4. As long as she can manage and always look beautiful, appearances don't matter.
  5. Have a good sense of humour and a great smile.

5 Turn offs when looking for a partner

  1. When she have poor communication skills.
  2. Dishonest with me and has a habit of keeping things from me.
  3. Flirtatious with other men.
  4. When she keeps mentioning her ex to me.
  5. Very disrespectful towards me and a poor listener.
